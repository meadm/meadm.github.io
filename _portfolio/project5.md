Performed RNA-seq analyses from poorly-annotated, non-model organisms that frequently required refining annotations and building de novo transcriptomes to enable accurate quantification. Processed and organized the resulting expression data for systems biologists who incorporated the outputs into machine learning workflows that combined omic and phenotypic data. This work supported the identification of key insights and development of data-driven hypotheses across diverse biological contexts.
